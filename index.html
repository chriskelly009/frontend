Mental Health Matters

Mental health matters. This is true today more than ever. In This fast-paced ever changing world, our mental health can be overlooked or ignored. This shouldn't happen. Through mental health awareness, you and I can bring light to this sometimes debilitating health issue.

Some important areas of mental health awareness are

  • Reducing Stigma
  • Improving Access to Mental Health Clinics
  • Assistance with medication for those who can't afford it
  • Early intervention

Head to NAMI's website for more information on getting help or to see how you can help others with their mental health.
